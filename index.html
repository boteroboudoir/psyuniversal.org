Welcome to Universal Psychological Services, where each client matters. We provide thoughtful, individualized care in a calm and supportive environment.

Our Approach

We believe therapy should feel human — not clinical, rushed, or impersonal. Our work is grounded in evidence-based practice while honoring each client’s lived experience, values, and pace.

Collaboration, respect, and trust guide every step of the process.

Personalized Care

No two individuals are the same. Treatment and assessment are carefully tailored to meet your specific needs and goals.

How We Help

We support children, adolescents, and adults with concerns related to mental health, learning, behavior, and emotional regulation.
