Our Services

Drug and Alcohol Abuse

At Universal Psychological Services LLC, we offer outpatient treatment services to those suffering from the disease of addiction. We strive to empower our clients as they learn to manage their struggles.
Parent–School Consultation

Empirical evidence shows that family engagement in education matters for student success. Family engagement improves school readiness, student achievement, and social skills. We offer parent–teacher consultation services to bolster student success through communication.
Executive Function Coaching

At Universal Psychological Services, we provide therapeutic support for children, adolescents, and adults to enhance executive functioning — a key skill set essential to optimal performance across life domains.
Psychological Evaluations

Grounded in psychological theory with attention to family systems, our psychological evaluations focus on understanding individual functioning within relational and developmental contexts.
Neuropsychological Evaluations

We collaborate closely with clients to understand patterns of behavior, cognition, and emotional functioning through comprehensive neuropsychological evaluation.
Forensic Evaluations for Family Court Custody Cases

Our forensic evaluations consider life transitions, chronic illness, identity, and belief systems as they relate to family court and custody matters.
Play Therapy

Play therapy provides an opportunity to explore emotional development and early experiences, particularly supporting children and adolescents.
Biofeedback

Our specialists provide biofeedback services and are glad to offer second opinions to support informed and personalized care.
Cognitive Rehabilitation

Cognitive rehabilitation views healthcare as a comprehensive psychological process focused on understanding, recovery, and adaptive functioning.
